# Card Reader — Product Brief

## One-line pitch
A wallet-style app that tells you which credit card perks, credits, and rewards you have not used yet — and what to do next to unlock more value.

## Core user problem
People carry premium cards with annual fees, rotating benefits, travel credits, lounge access, welcome bonuses, and category multipliers — but most do not remember:
- what each card offers
- which benefits reset monthly, quarterly, or annually
- how close they are to spend thresholds
- which card is best for a given purchase

## Core product promise
Tell the user, simply and proactively:
- use this card now
- this perk is still available
- you are this close to earning something valuable

## MVP features
- add card flow
- wallet-style card stack
- card details with rewards balance
- unused perk tracking
- spend threshold progress
- recommendation feed

## Target feel
- inspired by Apple Wallet
- dark, elegant, tactile
- minimal text on the home screen
- more depth when a card is opened

## Open product questions
- how rewards balances are sourced
- how perk usage is inferred or synced
- what level of issuer integration is feasible for v1
- how much personalization is manual vs automatic
